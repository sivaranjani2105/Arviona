# Arviona Master Product Rules

You must follow the **ARVIONA MASTER PRODUCT PROMPT** specifications when designing, modifying, or building modules in this workspace.

## Core Vision
* Transform traditional education into a personalized, engaging, adaptive, and gamified learning journey.
* **NOT** a traditional LMS: Replace boring layouts with game-like engagement (Duolingo for School).
* Learning flow transformation:
  * Lessons ➔ **Missions**
  * Assignments ➔ **Quests**
  * Exams/Tests ➔ **Boss Battles**
  * Marks ➔ **Mastery & Growth**

## Primary User Personas & Workflows
1. **Student**: Engaging dashboard with XP progress, Streaks, House rankings, evolving Learning Pet, and personalized Daily Missions.
2. **Teacher**: View student insights, performance trends, risk prediction models, and manage assignments converted to quests.
3. **Parent**: High-level visual indices ("Child Growth Score", "Consistency Streaks", Strength/Weakness alerts) rather than complex spreadsheets.
4. **School Administrator**: Full control to customize XP tables, evolve pets, configure badge requirements, set house rules, and adjust algorithms.
5. **Super Administrator**: Platform-wide settings, multi-tenancy configurations, and global defaults.

## Core Gamification Engine
* **XP System**: Actions award configurable XP values (Watch Lecture = 20, Quiz = 50, AI Tutor = 5, etc.).
* **Streak System**: Track and reward daily consistency (3, 7, 15, 30, 100, 365 days).
* **Badge System**: Admins configure custom badges with unique icons, descriptions, and criteria.
* **House System**: Internal school competition with point rankings, custom houses, and score tables.
* **Learning Pet System**: Virtual companions (Lion, Dragon, Panda, Owl, Penguin, Tiger) that hatch and evolve through stages (Baby, Junior, Advanced, Elite, Legendary) as students complete learning tasks.

## AI & Knowledge Map Systems
* **Arivo AI Tutor**: A mentor, coach, friend, and tutor that explains concepts, gives hints, and adapts tone based on age.
* **Knowledge Map**: Track mastery status (*Mastered*, *Learning*, *Weak*, *Critical*) at Subject, Chapter, Topic, and Subtopic levels.
* **Adaptive Learning Engine**: Personalize questions, recommendations, revision schedules, and daily missions based on engagement, tolerance, style, and study habit analysis.
